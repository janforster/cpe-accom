About the House

Located in Forest-Side, Curepipe, this two-floor townhouse is ideal for families and small groups. The area is safe, central, and close to shops, supermarkets, and nature spots like Trou aux Cerfs.

The ground floor features a bright, open-plan living, dining, and kitchen space. The first floor includes a full bathroom, a main bedroom, and a semi-private bedroom with a king-size bed – ideal for parents with children.

Key Features

  • Sleep up to 5 (king bed, queen + single bunk)
  • Wi-Fi and filtered drinking water
  • Dehumidifier, water pump, and gas stove
  • Washing machine, baby gates, and high chair
  • Secure expanda gate and parking next to the house
  • Access to rooftop (currently unfurnished)
  • Fully equipped kitchen with cooking essentials
