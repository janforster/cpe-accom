About the House

Located in Forest-Side, Curepipe, this two-floor townhouse is ideal for families and small groups. The area is safe, central, and close to shops, supermarkets, and nature spots like Trou aux Cerfs.

The ground floor features a bright, open-plan living, dining, and kitchen space. The first floor includes a full bathroom, one main bedroom, and a semi-private pass-through room with a king-size bed (separated by a curtain on one side) – ideal for parents with children.

Key Features

  • Sleeps up to 5 (king bed + queen/single bunk)
  • Reliable Wi-Fi and filtered drinking water
  • Dehumidifier, water pump, and gas stove
  • Washing machine; baby gates & high chair on request
  • Secure expanda gate; parking next to the house
  • Roof access (currently unfurnished)
  • Fully equipped kitchen with cooking essentials
